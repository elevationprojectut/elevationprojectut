A MINISTRY PROJECT IN COOPERATION WITH:

“Elevation Project”NAB-NW Association Nate Fox - Director
&
“International Christian Fellowship of Utah”ICF-Utah Bud Fuchs - NAB X-Cultural Equipper

WHO:

Teams of 4-10 people who want to see what God is doing in Utah, and experience what ministry to LDS( Latter Day Saints) looks like.

WHERE:

  • Utah “Life Elevated” – elevation ranges from 4,600-5,000 feet altitude
  • Primary ministry location is Utah County, Utah
  • 600,000+ population with approximately 85% in the Mormon faith.
  • Less than 1% of the population attends a Christian church
  • Two major universities with over 70,000 students – Utah Valley Univ. & Brigham Young Univ.

WHAT:

  • Serve together with “Elevation Project” alongside of Utah churches and ICF-Utah ministry
  • On-site Training
  • Tours of SLC & Provo
  • Dialogue with LDS people
  • Serve local churches with ministry events, kids programs, literature distribution, or construction & work projects.
  • Prayer walks on the UVU and BYU campuses
  • International Ministry events

PREPARATION & ORIENTATION:

  • Pre-trip Training - utahministry.org
  • Understanding Mormon/LDS faith and culture
  • Nate Fox – Utah Orientation. Understanding Mormon Culture and local Church connections
  • Bud Fuchs – Ministry to Internationals

WHEN:

  • All Year Round
  • 3rd weekend of the month for International ministry

COST:

  • Transportation to and from Utah
  • Van rentals in UT affordablerentacarandsales.com
  • Lodging: airbnb.com | Wood Spring Suites
  • Food: plan for $15-20/day per person

OTHER:

Utah Attractions:

  • LAGOON: great amusement park for much less cost than most
  • NATIONAL PARKS: 5 within 5 hour drive of Utah County
  • THANKSGIVING POINT: family friendly attractions
  • SKIUTAH: "Greatest Snow on Earth!”
  • TIMPONOGOS CAVE: amazing cave in the heart of the mountain is only 20 minutes away.
